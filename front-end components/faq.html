Frequently Asked Questions

What does Next-Gen QA do?

Next-Gen QA analyzes the elements of your website—such as buttons, links, input fields, and paragraphs—to automatically generate usability test cases that help ensure your site is functional for all users.

How long does it take to generate test cases?

It depends on your website’s complexity. On average, it takes about 5 minutes to generate one test case. Sites with more elements take longer, while simpler ones allow faster generation.

Who can use Next-Gen QA?

Anyone! Whether you're a student, software tester, QA engineer, or developer, Next-Gen QA helps streamline usability testing without requiring extensive technical knowledge.

Which websites can Next-Gen QA analyze?

Currently, Next-Gen QA supports .org, .edu, and .gov websites. We plan to expand support for more domains in the future!

What format are the test cases available in?

You can download test cases in CSV format for easy integration with testing tools, or export tables as a PDF for documentation and reporting purposes.

Is Next-Gen QA meant to replace QA professionals?

Not at all! Next-Gen QA is a tool to assist QA teams and testers, not replace them. It automates test case generation to save time and provide a solid starting point, but manual review and refinement are always recommended.
